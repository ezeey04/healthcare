Health Tips

Curated advice for your wellbeing

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to maintain healthy organ function and energy levels.

Prioritise Sleep

Adults need 7–9 hours of quality sleep. Poor sleep is linked to heart disease, obesity, and low immunity.

Move Every Day

A 30-minute brisk walk each day reduces risk of diabetes, heart disease, and boosts mental health.

Regular Checkups

Don't skip annual health screenings — early detection is the key to effective treatment.

Eat Balanced Meals

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in every meal for sustained energy.
